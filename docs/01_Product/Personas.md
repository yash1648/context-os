# User Personas

---

## Persona 1: Alex — The Knowledge Worker

> "I need a single place to track everything I read, watch, and learn."

**Demographics:**
- Age: 34
- Role: Technical Writer / Researcher
- Location: Remote
- Tech Savvy: High
- Devices: MacBook Pro, iPad, iPhone

**Goals:**
- Track 50+ books/articles read per year
- Organize research across multiple projects
- Save and categorize web clippings
- Retrieve information quickly when writing

**Pain Points:**
- Uses 5+ separate tools for different content types
- Forgets where she saved important information
- No unified search across her knowledge base
- Overwhelmed by bookmarks and notes spread across apps

**Behavior:**
- Reads 2-3 books simultaneously
- Saves 10+ articles per week
- Writes documentation with frequent reference lookups
- Prefers keyboard shortcuts and fast workflows

**ContextOS Superpowers:**
- Universal container model for all content types
- Semantic search across entire knowledge base
- Browser extension for one-click saves
- AI summaries for quick recall

## Persona 6: Dr. Wei — The Academic Researcher

> "I need to manage hundreds of papers, notes, and research threads."

**Demographics:**
- Age: 45
- Role: Professor / Researcher
- Location: Boston
- Tech Savvy: Medium-High
- Devices: Windows Desktop, iPad, iPhone

**Goals:**
- Organize 200+ research papers
- Track reading progress for each paper
- Connect findings across research areas
- Maintain annotated bibliographies

**Pain Points:**
- Reference managers are citation-focused, not context-focused
- Notes are disconnected from papers
- Research threads become hard to follow over years
- No AI assistance for literature review

**Behavior:**
- Reads 5-10 papers per week
- Takes extensive margin notes
- Collaborates with 3+ research groups
- Publishes 4-6 papers per year

**ContextOS Superpowers:**
- Knowledge Asset containers for paper annotations
- Knowledge Graph for research topic connections
- AI summarization for literature review
- Goal containers for research milestones
